About Me

I am an MS student in the Aerospace Engineering Sciences department at CU Boulder. I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor of Science in Economics and a Bachelor of Applied Science in Computer Science and Mathematics (minor). Prior to pursuing graduate studies, I worked as a software engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS) for three years.

My MS research focuses on advancing autonomous systems using machine learning techniques that enable them to operate efficiently in novel and uncertain situations.
